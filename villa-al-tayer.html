VILLA AL TAYER

Home is a place that provides us with a centering - a place from which we leave each morning and return to each evening.

The spaces we live in have a fundamental role to play in everyday life and well being - they give meaning to our experience and we construct an endearing bond with them.

Villa Al Tayer has been carefully designed to reflect belonging and refuge... a sense of safe space.

Villa Al Tayer reflects classic simplicity in the balance of authentic materials with natural light. Clean, simple and balanced.

Consistent and creative design creates a haven from the stresses of the external world.
